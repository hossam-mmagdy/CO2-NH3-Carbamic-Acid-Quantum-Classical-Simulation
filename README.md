# CO2-NH3-Carbamic-Acid-Quantum-Classical-Simulation
Ab initio quantum-classical simulation of CO₂+NH₃→carbamic acid: PySCF electronic structure (HF/DFT/MP2/CCSD(T)), potential energy surface scanning, and VQE quantum computing with IBM Eagle noise modeling. Features 13 publication-quality figures analyzing reaction thermodynamics, transition states, and NISQ-era quantum chemistry feasibility.
